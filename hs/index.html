Our Vision

We are a reference point at the national level, leaders in the educational community, recognized for the high quality of our services, acting with social sensitivity, and committed to a culture of excellence and success in life.

Enrollment

Enrollment is open year-around. Call, email or visit the school office for more information.

Scholarships

Full and partial scholarships for academic and artistic excellence are available to students who have been enrolled in the school for at least one year.

Our Mission

Hughes Schools is a bilingual educational institution whose mission it is to train and educate students of integrity and accomplishment, using the tools of academic and artistic excellence in an atmosphere of safety and security. We have a committed team of specialized professionals who are continuously researching and updating teaching methods appropriate for the constantly changing educational environment in order to respond to the needs of students, society, and the nation. We maintain excellent communication with our students and all sectors of the global community based on respect and mutual understanding.

Major Awards and Academic Distinctions

Desire is the key to motivation, but it is determination and commitment to an unrelenting pursuit of your goal - a commitment to excellence - that will enable you to attain the success you seek.
Mario Andretti


Hughes Schools students have been awarded more than 160 individual distinctions for Academic Excellence, including the following:

National Youth Science Foundation Scholarship

Sponsored by the United States Department of State and the National Youth Science Foundation, this scholarship is awarded to academically talented students to develop their highest potential in the sciences. The month-long program takes place in the state of West Virginia and the scholarship includes all travel and living expenses.

National Space School (NASA) Scholarship

Students receive a full scholarship to attend the United Space School in Houston, Texas. Scholarship winners participate in a variety of space-related activities at the Johnson Space Center, the University of Houston Clear Lake, and Space Center Houston. Activities promote science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) studies and international collaborations.

C-tech2 Scholarship at Virginia Tech University

C-Tech2 is held on the campus of Virginia Tech University in Blacksburg, Virginia. Students participate in a wide-variety of engineering activities and projects and attend informational sessions on topics such as college admissions and scholarships, career planning and the engineering majors.

Ibero-americano, National, State, and District Physics and Mathematics Olympics Awards

Over 100 Hughes Schools students have received Gold and Silver medals in a wide range of theoretical exam competitions sponsored by the Universidad Mayor de San Simon and the Government of Bolivia.

Performance Opportunities

Over 200 national and international Hughes Schools performances include:

International Dance Festivals

Recent participation has included the Sestu International Folk Festival in Sestu, Italy, the Friendship Folk Festival in Iquique, Chile, and the Magic Valley Folk Festival, World Folkfest, and Summerfest Folk Festival in the states of Utah and Idaho, in the United States (July and August 2016).

Profile of a Graduate of Hughes Schools

The Hughes Schools graduate is an ordinary person who does extraordinary things.

He or she is a leader who in thought, voice, and action applies positive values and personal knowledge for self-growth and to improve the world.

With solid logical, critical, and creative thinking, the Hughes Schools graduate sets high goals and reaches them with excellence.

Thoughtful, active, and productive, he or she has the tools to achieve anything at any time and uses these abilities to the great social benefit of family, friends, community, and country.

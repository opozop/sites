Our School

Hughes Schools provides a comprehensive, high quality education from Prekindergarten to High School with special emphasis on English, university level science and mathematics, and pre-conservatory training in music and dance.

 

Our Vision

We are a reference point at the national level, leaders in the educational community, recognized for the high quality of our services, acting with social sensitivity, and committed to a culture of excellence and success in life.

Details »

Our mission

Hughes Schools is a bilingual educational institution whose mission it is to train and educate students of integrity and accomplishment, using the tools of academic and artistic excellence in an atmosphere of safety and security. We have a committed team of specialized professionals who are continuously researching and updating teaching methods appropriate for the constantly changing educational environment in order to respond to the needs of students, society, and the nation. We maintain excellent communication with our students and all sectors of the global community based on respect and mutual understanding.

Profile of a Graduate of Hughes Schools
The Hughes Schools graduate is an ordinary person who does extraordinary things. He or she is a leader who in thought, voice, and action applies positive values and personal knowledge for self-growth and to improve the world. With solid logical, critical, and creative thinking, the Hughes Schools graduate sets high goals and reaches them with excellence. Thoughtful, active, and productive, he or she has the tools to achieve anything at any time and uses these abilities to the great social benefit of family, friends, community, and country.

Academics

The Hughes Schools Academic Program consists of preschool, elementary, and secondary schools organized on a daily schedule of six 45-minute classes for all grade levels. PERFORMING ARTS

  • Students in elementary grades 1 to 4 take a preparatory program of , , , and
  • choir
  • music theory
  • jazz/ballet dance
  • Bolivian Folk dance

  • Students specializing in Music take classes in
  • music theory
  • choir
